About Me

Hello! I'm Bradley Rising-Tchernucha, a recent Biomedical Science graduate from Edge Hill University who has transitioned into web development. I specialize in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, and Redux, and have hands-on experience with Node.js and Git. My front-end development skills have been honed through comprehensive coursework on Codecademy via The Learning People. Before diving into tech, I was a certified English teacher, teaching abroad and embracing diverse cultures. Outside of coding, I'm passionate about athletics, which has instilled in me a strong sense of discipline and perseverance. I am committed to building responsive and user-friendly web applications and thrive in collaborative environments. Feel free to explore my portfolio or reach out for potential collaborations!
